# Introduction

Welcome to the EarnKit documentation!

EarnKit is a monetization toolkit that lets developers add usage-based billing to AI agents **and** automatically convert a portion of that revenue into buy pressure for their token — on-chain, with zero manual steps.

## What Makes EarnKit Different

Most monetization platforms stop at payments. EarnKit goes further with **Auto Buyback & Burn** — every credit purchase your users make can automatically trigger a token buyback and burn on Base. You set a percentage, pick a threshold, and EarnKit handles the rest: fee collection, swapping via Uniswap V3, and burning. No cron jobs. No multisig. No manual execution.

**Platform fee: 0%.** You keep 100% of revenue (minus whatever you choose to allocate to buyback).

## The Full Feature Set

- **Reliable Billing** — the `track`/`capture`/`release` pattern ensures users are never charged for a failed AI response.
- **Multi-Token Payments** — users top up with ETH, USDC, or MINIDEV on Base. Payment logic is server-side; your client just signs a transaction.
- **Auto Buyback & Burn** — route a configurable percentage of every top-up into an automated on-chain buyback pipeline for your token. [Learn more →](./buyback-and-burn)
- **Off-Chain Speed** — per-prompt charges happen in the database for instant, gas-free billing.
- **Developer Dashboard** — manage agents, configure fee models, view usage logs, and monitor buyback executions.
