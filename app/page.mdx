# Introduction

Welcome to the EarnKit documentation.

EarnKit is a **token buyback-as-a-service** platform. Connect any on-chain revenue stream — splits contracts, fee recipients, or token contracts — to fully automated buyback & burn, with zero manual steps.

## What EarnKit Does

When on-chain fees accumulate in your buyback wallet, EarnKit automatically swaps them to your target token via Uniswap V3 and burns them. No cron jobs. No multisig. No manual execution.

**Platform fee: 5% per execution** — deducted from the swap input before the buyback runs.

## How It Works

Connect any existing **0xSplits V2** contract (Clanker, Doppler, or custom) to automated buyback & burn. EarnKit provisions a Turnkey-managed wallet, you set it as the fee recipient on your splits contract, and revenue automatically flows into token burns.

→ [Full details](./buyback-and-burn)

## How to Get Started

1. Go to [app.earnkit.com](https://app.earnkit.com) and sign in.
2. Navigate to **Create Buybacks** in the sidebar.
3. Paste your splits contract address and follow the setup flow.
4. Set the provisioned wallet as the fee recipient on your contract.
5. Revenue accumulates and triggers automatic buyback & burn once the threshold is crossed.

## Architecture Overview

```
Fee revenue → Splits contract / Fee recipient
                 └── EarnKit buyback wallet (Turnkey-managed)
                          └── threshold crossed?
                               ├── yes → deduct 5% platform fee
                               │         → swap remaining → your token → burn
                               └── no  → accumulates
```

- **Non-custodial** — you remain the owner of your splits or token contract at all times. The buyback wallet is only the fee recipient.
- **Immutable at registration** — the buyback wallet address is baked in at setup time. There is no admin key that can redirect funds.
- **Operator gas** — EarnKit drips a small ETH reserve before each execution so the buyback wallet always has gas. Gas is excluded from the swappable balance check.
